Roti Jala Coklat - 3 cawan tepung gandum
- 2 biji telur
- 3 sudu besar serbuk koko
- 3 sudu besar gula pasir
- Sedikit garam ( sis guna garam bukit satu sudu teh )
- 3 sudu besar minyak jagung @ minyak zaitun
- 1/2 cawan susu cair
- 1 1/2 cawan air ( boleh tambah sekiranya adunan nampak pekat )
 1. Masukkan ke semua bahan ke dalam blender dan blend hingga halus.
2. Adunan tak pekat dan tak cair sangat boleh sedia untuk menjala.
3. Tuangkan adunan ke dalam botol kaki tiga.
4. Guna kuali non stick dan start menjala sampai habis.

Aiskrim Goreng - Roti gardenia
- 1 bekas aiskrim
 Cara penyediaan :
1. Ambil sekeping roti dan tekan bahagian tengah roti dengan
sudu. Letakkan satu sudu aiskrim pada bahagian roti yang telah ditekan tadi.
2. Ambil sekeping roti yang baru dan letak diatas roti yang mempunyai
aiskrim tadi. Ambil cawan dan tekap diatas roti tersebut. Pastikan cawan
tersebut tidak tekap diatas aiskrim.
3. Pastikan roti tersebut ditekap dengan kuat agar aiskrim tidak keluar dari roti.
4. Keluarkan bahagian kulit roti yang berlebihan disekeliling cawan tersebut.
(ulang langkah 1 hingga langkah 4 sebanyak mana anda mahukan).
5. Bagi mengelakkan aiskrim cair, roti yang telah siap dibuat tadi lebih
baik diletakkan di dalam peti sejuk beku sementara menyiapkan roti asikrim yang lain.
6. apabila sudah cukup kuantiti yang dikehendaki, goreng roti tersebut di
dalam minyak yang betul-betul panas dalam masa beberapa saat sahaja bagi
mengelakkan roti aiskrim cair.
7. Aiskrim roti sudah sedia untuk dihidangkan. Lebih sedap makan
terus selepas digoreng.


The humble notebook, Buku 555 has given a boost into the 21st century by turning it into an app. Just like its paper counterpart, Buku 555 is a simple, no-frills app with just enough features to help you keep a record of important notes, data or memo. This app is divided into several sections such as notes, studies, expenses and recipes. Each of these parts has a bit of its own twist.



The first menu in the navigation bar is notes. In the navigation section of the note, the user can save information such as passwords and usernames or the like for all the applications that the user has.



The second menu is studies. In the navigation studies section, the user can save the name of the subject and the link of the note to make it easier for the user to review the notes when needed or to review the lesson.



Then is the expenses menu. In this section, users can record all daily expenses as well as total expenses. This can indirectly make it easier for users to plan their finances and review past expenses.



The last menu is the recipes menu. In this section, users can review recipes that have been mentioned to make it easier for users to cook foods that they do not know. The user can also see a picture of the food if the user does not know the food. The method of preparation is also described in this section of the recipe.
