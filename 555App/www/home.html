The humble notebook, Buku 555 has given a boost into the 21st century by turning it into an app.

Just like its paper counterpart, Buku 555 is a simple, no-frills app with just enough features to help you keep a record of important notes, data or memo.

This app is divided into several sections such as notes, studies, expenses and recipes. Each of these parts has a bit of its own twist.
